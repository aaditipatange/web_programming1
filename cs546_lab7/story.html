The story goes like this...

A little girl who started her life in a place where she was nurtured and cared for by
her parents to the point that she loved her comfort more than anything,
my Dad has always been a supporting anchor no matter what I chose for myself,
He has given me immense love and support to make me the confident girl I am today,
my drive for a career in the technology sector has been there since the start,
not very unusual for the daughter
of a businessman and a homemaker to be a tech enthusiast.
Although my parents don't belong to the same field as me but their support has made me more
driven to achieve my goals and follow my dream for masters in the United States,
a hub and centre for technology.

A masters degree in the USA has been a dream for many,
unfortunately not many can achieve it due to many reasons.
Coming to the US has in itself been a dream come true,
coming to a new city had its own excitement.
For a girl who never travelled abroad and then coming to a city so
close to the New York City, the world's capital,
has had a different energy around me.
I wished only for the best university for my masters,
and getting an admit from the esteemed Stevens Institute of Technology got me a lot closer to my dream.

Well this is my story as of yet but a lot more to unveil in my
journey over the next many years to come.
I am going to be only optimistic about what life has to present be it something as
frivolous as getting my nails done or something major like landing
my dream job and getting my dream job, because negativity
makes life's experiences very cumbersome.
